Introduction

Philosophy studies fundamental questions concerning human existence, thought, knowledge, reason, and beyond. Socrates is considered the “father” of Philosophy, as he popularized it and expanded views that can now be branched out to many categories. His relentless pursuit of truth and unwavering commitment to questioning paved the way for centuries of intellectual exploration.

This website offers a short guide through the three of the most renowned philosophical movements. From the ancient wisdom of Plato to the existential ponderings of Nietzsche, each movement offers unique insights into the human condition and the nature of reality.

In the Content section, you will find introductions to the movements, in the Library section, there are recommended readings, and in the Form section, you can leave feedback.
